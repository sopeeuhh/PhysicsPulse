PhysicsPulse is an international nonprofit youth organization that aims to provide content in an engaging way that challenges the sterotypes surrounding physics. Our goal is to help you see the physics around you from a new perspective—and appreciate it in your everyday life.

About the Founder

Hi, I'm Fiona Lee, and I'm a physics enthusiast!

During the pandemic, I started volunteering online a lot and quickly discovered how much I love giving back. Later on in high school, I developed a deep interest in physics, but I noticed that not many people saw it the way I did. A lot of students find it intimidating or boring and never get the chance to truly enjoy it.

That's why I created Physics Pulse: to share my love for physics, break the sterotype that it's unapproachable, give others a chance to volunteer for a meaningful cause and connect people from all over the world who share the same passion.
